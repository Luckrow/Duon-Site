About

Cracks in space are showing up out of nowhere, and strange creatures are wreaking havoc across two parallel universes. Take control of two characters, Mark Rikward and Luna Chaser, each in a separate universe, and embark on a mission to find the cause of this chaos.

Duon: Project Worlds is a combo-focused 2D action RPG, inspired by games like Odin Sphere and Dust: an Elysian Tail. In Duon, you will explore locations such as overrun cities, noxious swamps and a rift between dimensions, fighting hundreds of different enemies in the form of Outmarrow, a relentless

pack of instinct-driven creatures, and gathering clues in hopes of stopping these bizarre events and saving both universes.

THE DEVELOPERS

Pulsar Games is a soon-to-be games studio, formed and kept together by the hardships of college. Currently composed of six members, our team has been hard at work creating the art, music and code that will culminate into Duon. You can follow our work on social media (links below) and keep up to date on our progress on the game.

DUON PROJECT WORLDS

Duon is Pulsar's first team project. It started as just a narrative, created by our writer, quickly grew into the idea of an RPG with duality as its driving theme, and is now being developed as a full-fledged 2D action RPG.
